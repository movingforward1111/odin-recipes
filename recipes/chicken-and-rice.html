chicken and rice

Best homemade rice and chicken you can make!

This rice and chicken recipe includes savory mouth watering seasoned cajun chicken with delcious steamed white rice

Ingredients

  • 28g of chicken
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of extra virgin olive oil
  • 5 tsp of cajun seasoning

Steps

  1. You're going to want to start by pouring a cup and half of water into a pot and let it boil
  2. As you let the water boil, start getting your chicken(make sure you have 28g!) ready and season your chicken front and back with the cajun seasoning
  3. As you get done with the chicken start pouring your tsp of extra virgin oilve oil onto a pan and let it get upto temp.
  4. After waiting for the water to boil and and oil to be upto to temp. get a cup of rice and put it into the boiling water and drop the temp about a third. Then take your chicken and and put it on the stove pan
  5. Let the rice boil for 9 minutes and then put it to the side
  6. For the chicken you're going to want to cook each side for 5 minutes twice and then you're able to put it to the side
  7. After everything is cooked you're going to want to use some meat shears and cut the chicken into bits in a bowl. once thats done just top it of with the white rice, give it a good mix and enjoy!
